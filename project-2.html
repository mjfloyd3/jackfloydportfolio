Financial research report templates

Design and development of custom research authoring workflow for Institutional Investment Banking clients

BlueMatrix

Role: Designer & Developer

Background

I design and develop custom-branded research templates used by investment banks to author and publish research across multiple formats.

These templates function as the foundation of each client's research workflow, allowing analysts to focus on content while ensuring outputs are accurate, compliant, and visually consistent with each firm's brand.

My role sits at the intersection of design systems and front-end engineering. I translate each client's branding, layout needs, and data requirements into flexible authoring templates, then implement them directly within their publishing environment.

On the technical side, the web outputs dynamically generate XML based on client-specific configurations, pulling in structured financial and company data from internal models and their admin setup. I use XSLT to transform this data into production-ready layouts, ensuring information appears correctly and consistently across all formats.

This work supports hundreds of clients simultaneously, requiring solutions that are scalable, maintainable, and adaptable to different branding rules, data structures, and publishing constraints.

Process

Investment banks publish complex, data-heavy research consistently across formats while maintaining strict brand standards and produce high-stakes research that must:

  • Be accurate and compliant
  • Match firm-specific branding
  • One-click publishing reliably across web, PDF, email, etc.

Constraints

  • Enterprise fintech environment with strict publishing and compliance requirements
  • Non-technical authors creating content inside a structured authoring system
  • Multiple output formats with different layout and rendering limitations
  • Client-specific data models powering financial tables, company information, and metrics
  • Scalability: solutions must work across dozens of clients without becoming brittle

Solution

A scalable, design-driven template system that bridges authoring, data, and output.

I design and implement custom authoring templates that:

  • Encode each client's brand system (typography, spacing, hierarchy, tone)
  • Guide analysts toward consistent, readable layouts
  • Dynamically pull in structured financial and company data
  • Render reliably across web, PDF, and email outputs
  • Scalability: solutions must work across dozens of clients without becoming brittle

The result is a flexible system where:

  • Authors focus on content
  • Design remains consistent
  • Data flows accurately from internal models to published research

Impact

  • Enabled dozens of investment banks and boutique research firms to publish branded research at scale
  • Reduced manual formatting errors and inconsistencies
  • Improved cross-format consistency between web, PDF, and email outputs
  • Created reusable widgets across client sites that optimized onboardings for new clients while providing smaller firms without design/marketing teams that informed how to best structure their content
