About me

With a background in both design and front-end development, I bring equal attention to how things look, how they work, and how they're built.

Since 2018 I've been designing and building products at BlueMatrix, a SaaS fintech firm powering the global investment research industry. My work there spans UI design, front-end development, and everything in between. I own projects from early wireframes to shipped code.

Before BlueMatrix, a GIS certification sparked my interest in design. Learning to turn complex spatial data into clear, readable visuals was where I first understood what good design is capable of.
